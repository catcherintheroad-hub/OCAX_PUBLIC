## 9. Overall Profile Style Guide

### Do

- ✅ Keep it simple
- ✅ Use everyday words
- ✅ Focus on money/income angle
- ✅ Mention "real tasks"
- ✅ Make it feel like a real product
- ✅ Use short sentences

### Don't

- ❌ Don't say "AI verification"
- ❌ Don't mention "blockchain"
- ❌ Don't use "node" or "validator"
- ❌ Don't sound like a scam
- ❌ Don't overpromise
- ❌ Don't use technical jargon

### Tone

- Friendly
- Clear
- Direct
- Honest about being early stage
- Focused on value exchange
