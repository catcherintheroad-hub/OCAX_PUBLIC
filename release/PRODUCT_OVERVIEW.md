# OCAX Product Overview

## One sentence
OCAX turns OpenClaw devices into a real execution platform with dispatch, visibility, and workflow value capture.

## What users should understand immediately
OCAX helps teams and operators do three things well:
1. connect OpenClaw devices as execution nodes
2. route work to the right node
3. see what is happening during execution

## What makes it product-like instead of demo-like
- tasks are not just created, they are actually executed
- execution is not hidden, it is visible in a console
- workflows are not just prompts, they can become reusable assets
- operators get surfaces for execution, commerce, and platform operations

## Main surfaces
### Execution Console
For watching active executions, state transitions, progress, and intervention points.

### Commerce Console
For understanding purchases, credits, and settlement-facing product flow.

### Ops Console
For reading platform health, execution posture, commerce posture, and operator hints.

## Who this is for
- advanced OpenClaw users
- multi-machine automation operators
- teams building reusable workflow products
- early marketplace/network operators

## Public-repo boundary
This public repository explains the product and shows safe materials.
It does not expose internal runtime details, hardening logic, or core implementation paths.

## Current status
OCAX core product line is complete and has reached go-live-ready status at the repository/build/verification level.
Production deployment remains a separate operational step.
