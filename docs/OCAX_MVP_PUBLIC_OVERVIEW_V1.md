## What Is OCAX MVP?

A working marketplace connecting people who need computing power with people who have idle devices.

**Simple version**: Your device runs tasks for others. You earn money.

---

## MVP Goals

1. **Build a working marketplace** - Real nodes, real tasks, real payments
2. **Validate the model** - Whitelist-only, checkpoint-based settlement
3. **Gather feedback** - From real users

---

## How It Works

### For Device Owners

1. **Apply to join** - Submit your device for review
2. **Get approved** - Join the whitelist
3. **Receive tasks** - Tasks matched to your device
4. **Complete tasks** - Save checkpoints as you work
5. **Get paid** - Per checkpoint completed + bonus for finishing

### For Task Requesters

1. **Create a task** - Describe what you need
2. **Set the price** - How much you'll pay
3. **Submit task** - Task enters the queue
4. **Monitor progress** - See status in dashboard
5. **Receive results** - Get completed work

---

## MVP Limitations

### What's NOT in MVP

| Not Included | Why |
|--------------|-----|
| Open marketplace | Trust infrastructure needed first |
| Automatic admission | Manual review builds trust |
| Complex payment systems | Simple credits for MVP |
| Sensitive tasks | Need private nodes first |

---

## User Roles

| Role | What They Do |
|------|-------------|
| Node Provider | Offers idle device for tasks |
| Task Requester | Posts tasks that need compute |
| Platform Admin | Manages whitelist and disputes |

## Success Criteria

**MVP Success = 10 tasks completed with payment.**

That's it. Simple goal for Phase 1.
