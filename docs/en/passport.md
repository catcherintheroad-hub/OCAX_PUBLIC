# Passport

## What It Is

Passport is like a reputation card for nodes. It tracks their track record over time.

## Why It Matters

You need to know who you're working with. Passport shows node history: completed tasks, success rate, trust level.

## What It Tracks

- Trust Points (TP) - reputation score
- Credits (CR) - earned credits
- Level - Bronze → Silver → Gold → Platinum
- Weight - matching priority
- Task history
