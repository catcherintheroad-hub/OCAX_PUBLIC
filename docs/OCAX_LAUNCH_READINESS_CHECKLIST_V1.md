## Show HN

### Ready Now?

**❌ NOT READY**

### Why

Show HN is for:
- Working products
- Something people can try
- Real demos

We have:
- Concept verified ✅
- Basic execution ✅
- Nothing to try ❌

### What's Missing

- Live demo
- Working API
- User interface

### When Ready

- When there's something interactive to show
- Maybe 2-4 weeks after launch

---

## Product Hunt

### Ready Now?

**❌ NOT READY**

### Why

Product Hunt requires:
- Complete product page
- Clear value proposition
- Screenshots/video
- Launch team

We have:
- Basic repo ✅
- Not a "product" yet ❌

### What's Missing

- Product page
- Screenshots
- Launch team
- Clear product story

### When Ready

- When v1.0 is ready
- When there's a real product to show
- Maybe 2-3 months

---

## Reddit

### Ready Now?

**❌ NOT READY**

### Why

Reddit hates:
- Pure promotional posts
- "We're launching" content
- Spam

Reddit loves:
- Genuine discussion
- Technical deep dives
- Real problems

We have:
- Interesting concept ✅
- Not enough to discuss ❌

### What's Missing

- Technical deep dive content
- Discussion-worthy updates
- Community engagement plan

### When Ready

- When you have real traction to share
- When you can discuss technical details
- Maybe 1-2 months

---

## Summary

| Platform | Ready? | When |
|----------|---------|------|
| Show HN | ❌ Later | 2-4 weeks |
| Product Hunt | ❌ Later | 2-3 months |
| Reddit | ❌ Later | 1-2 months |

---

## What to Do First

1. **X/Twitter** - Build initial awareness
2. **GitHub** - Perfect the landing page
3. **Community** - Build small following
4. **Then** - Consider these platforms

---

## The Real Answer

These platforms are NOT for launch day. They're for AFTER you've built traction.

**Focus on X/Twitter and GitHub first.**
