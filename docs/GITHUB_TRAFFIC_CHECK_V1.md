## How to Check GitHub Traffic

### 1. View Traffic Insights

1. Go to your repository
2. Click "Insights" tab
3. Click "Traffic"

### 2. Key Metrics to Watch

| Metric | What It Shows |
|--------|---------------|
| **Visitors** | Unique people who viewed |
| **Views** | Total page views |
| **Clones** | How many people cloned |
| **Referring sites** | Where visitors came from |

---

## What to Check After Posting

### Day 1-3 After X Post

Check these:

1. **Visitors** - Did anyone click the link?
2. **Views** - Are people looking at the repo?
3. **Referring sites** - Is X/Twitter showing up?

### What Numbers Mean

| Metric | Good Sign |
|--------|------------|
| Visitors > 10 | Some interest |
| Visitors > 50 | Good traction |
| Referring sites includes X/Twitter | X is working |
| Clones > 0 | Someone might be trying |
