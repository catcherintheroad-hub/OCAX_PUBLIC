## Homepage Wording

### English

> **OCAX: Task verification for AI agents**
>
> Your AI agent says it completed the task. But can you prove it?
>
> OCAX verifies. Execution ≠ Completion. Validation = Completion.

### Chinese

> **OCAX：AI 任务验证网络**
>
> AI 代理说任务完成了。你能证明吗？
>
> OCAX 验证。执行 ≠ 完成。验证 = 完成。
